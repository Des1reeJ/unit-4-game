Crystal Collector Game


When a game is initialized, each jewel is assigned a number between 1 and 14. On every attempt, your task is to click one of the four dazzling jewel images, and they will increase the total score based on the assigned value each is given.



If the score you recieve coincides with the random number, you have numberical victory! However, if the score recieved exceeds numerical value, you lose!
